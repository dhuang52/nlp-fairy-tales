Correlation.

Interested in: How similar are English fairy tales?

Correlation is calculated using a bag of words model and then measuring the cosine similarity. So, a score of 0 means there is a weak correlation and 1 represents a strong correlation.

The grid below is a correlation matrix. The color of the circles represent the magnitude of the correlation. Try hovering over and clicking on the nodes of the matrix to learn more about correlation between two fairy tales!

  • Hovering will reveal a tooltip with the titles of the fairy tales under observation, the cosine similarity score (listed as "Similarity"), and the number of words shared between the texts.
  • Clicking will modify the text above. The listed words are the 5 most important words in the fairy tale determined by tf-idf scores, and the numbers next to the words are the tf-idf scores.
